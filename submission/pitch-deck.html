Executor

Autonomous meeting-to-execution enforcement.

Executor closes the gap between "we decided" and "it got done" by turning meetings into tracked commitments, risk signals, follow-ups, and manager escalations.

Voice to commitments Speechmatics captures live meetings and uploaded audio.
Reasoning layer Gemini extracts owners, dates, priorities, and evidence.
Execution loop Vultr monitor enforces overdue work every six hours.
AI Agent Olympics Hackathon 2026 executor.genesis-ai.workers.dev
Problem

Action items fail after the meeting, not during extraction.

The real gap Teams leave meetings with decisions, but ownership, deadlines, reminders, and escalation paths are scattered or missing.
Why summaries are not enough A summary is passive. Execution needs assignment, monitoring, risk scoring, tool sync, and accountable follow-through.
  • Commitments are buried across recordings, notes, and chat threads.
  • Project tools do not know which meeting created the work.
  • Managers discover stalled commitments after deadlines slip.

Solution

A five-agent pipeline that keeps work moving.

1
Ingestion Live Speechmatics transcription or transcript upload.
2
Extraction Gemini returns structured action items and source quotes.
3
Assignment Tasks sync into Jira, Asana, and Notion.
4
Monitoring Risk score updates as deadlines and status change.
5
Escalation Follow-ups and manager drafts with human review.

Architecture

Production-shaped stack, not a static demo.

Frontend React, TanStack Start, Tailwind, Cloudflare Workers deployment.
Data and auth Supabase Auth, Postgres tables, RLS-ready design, audit log.
AI reasoning Gemini Flash or Pro for extraction, risk, and email drafting.
Voice Speechmatics real-time transcription for live meetings.
Execution tools Jira, Asana, and Notion APIs create external task records.
Enforcement Vultr Ubuntu VM, PM2, cron schedule, monitor endpoint.
Executor pitch deck04
Autonomy

The monitor runs after people stop watching.

6h

The Vultr worker checks commitments every six hours and can also be triggered manually for demos.

  • Flags overdue or stalled items based on deadline and status.
  • Drafts owner follow-ups with context from the source meeting.
  • Escalates to a manager after repeated unanswered follow-ups.
  • Stores every action in the audit log for traceability.
